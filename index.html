人体解剖器官与结构词汇表
Vocabulary List of Human Anatomical Organs and Structures

本词汇表收录了人体主要器官及解剖结构的中英文术语，附带拼音和详细定义，适合医学、健康科学及双语教学使用。内容涵盖神经系统、循环系统、呼吸系统、消化系统、生殖与泌尿系统及牙齿等多个人体系统，帮助学习者全面掌握人体解剖相关词汇。

This vocabulary list compiles the key anatomical organs and structures of the human body with terms in Chinese and English, including Pinyin and detailed definitions. It is designed for use in medicine, health sciences, and bilingual education. The content covers multiple human body systems such as the nervous, circulatory, respiratory, digestive, reproductive & urinary systems, and teeth, helping learners develop a comprehensive understanding of human anatomy terminology.

🧠 主要器官 (Major Organs)

术语 (Term) 拼音 (Pinyin) 定义 (Definition)
Brain大脑
 dà nǎo
中枢神经系统的主要部分，控制思维、情绪、感觉和动作。The main part of the central nervous system that controls thinking, emotions, sensation, and movement.
Kidney肾脏
 shèn zàng
过滤血液、产生尿液，调节体液平衡和血压。Filters blood, produces urine, regulates fluid balance and blood pressure.
Liver肝脏
 gān zàng
解毒、合成蛋白质、储存能量及代谢营养。Detoxifies, synthesizes proteins, stores energy, and metabolizes nutrients.
Lungs肺
 fèi
进行气体交换，吸入氧气，排出二氧化碳。Performs gas exchange, inhaling oxygen and exhaling carbon dioxide.
Pancreas胰腺
 yí xiàn
分泌胰岛素、消化酶，调节血糖。Secretes insulin and digestive enzymes, regulating blood sugar.
Thyroid甲状腺
 jiǎ zhuàng xiàn
产生调节新陈代谢的激素。Produces hormones that regulate metabolism.
Heart心脏
 xīn zàng
泵血器官，推动血液循环。A pumping organ that drives blood circulation.
Spleen脾脏
 pí zàng
免疫和血液过滤功能，储存白细胞和血小板。Functions in immunity and blood filtration, storing white blood cells and platelets.

🧠 泌尿系统 (Urinary System)

术语 (Term) 拼音 (Pinyin) 定义 (Definition)

输送尿液从肾至膀胱的管道。Tubes that transport urine from the kidneys to the bladder.
Bladder膀胱

储存尿液的器官。The organ that stores urine.
Bladder Neck膀胱颈
 páng guāng jǐng
膀胱与尿道之间的连接处。The connection between the bladder and the urethra.
Urethra尿道
 niào dào
排尿通道，男性也用于排精。The tube that carries urine from the bladder to outside the body; also used for semen in males.
Adrenal gland肾上腺
 shèn shàng xiàn
位于肾上方，分泌应激激素。Located above the kidneys, secretes stress hormones.
Anus肛门
 gāng mén
排泄物排出体外的通道。The opening through which waste products are expelled from the body.

❤️ 循环与呼吸系统 (Circulatory & Respiratory Systems)

术语 (Term) 拼音 (Pinyin) 定义 (Definition)
Artery动脉
 dòng mài
将血液从心脏输送到全身的血管。Blood vessel that carries blood away from the heart to the rest of the body.
Atrium心房
 xīn fáng
心脏上部腔室，接收血液。Upper chamber of the heart that receives blood.
Blood vessel血管
 xuè guǎn
输送血液的通道，包括动脉、静脉和毛细血管。Channels that transport blood, including arteries, veins, and capillaries.
Bronchus支气管
 zhī qì guǎn
气管的分支，引导空气进入肺部。A branch of the trachea that leads air into the lungs.
Carina隆凸
 lóng tū
气管分成左右支气管的分叉点。The point where the trachea divides into the left and right main bronchi.
Chest Wall胸壁
 xiōng bì
包括肋骨、肌肉等结构，保护胸腔器官。Includes structures like ribs and muscles, protecting the thoracic organs.
Diaphragm隔膜
 gé mó
呼吸主要肌肉，分隔胸腹腔。The main muscle of respiration, separating the thoracic and abdominal cavities.
Hilum肺门
 fèi mén
血管、神经等进出肺部的部位。The area where blood vessels, nerves, etc., enter and exit the lungs.
Lymph nodes淋巴结
 lín bā jié
过滤淋巴液，参与免疫反应。Filter lymph fluid and participate in immune responses.
Lymph vessels淋巴管
 lín bā guǎn
运输淋巴液的管道。Vessels that transport lymph fluid.
Mediastinum纵膈腔
 zòng gé qiāng
胸腔中部区域，位于两肺之间。The central compartment of the thoracic cavity, located between the two lungs.
Pericardium心包
 xīn bāo
包裹心脏的膜，保护和润滑心脏。The membrane enclosing the heart, protecting and lubricating it.
Pleura胸膜
 xiōng mó
包裹肺部并减少摩擦的双层膜。A double-layered membrane enclosing the lungs and reducing friction.
Rib肋骨
 lèi gǔ
保护胸腔的重要骨骼结构。Important bony structures that protect the thoracic cavity.
Trachea气管
 qì guǎn
连接喉与支气管的呼吸道。The airway connecting the larynx to the bronchi.
Valve瓣膜
 bàn mó
控制血液单向流动的结构。A structure that controls unidirectional blood flow.
Vein静脉
 jìng mài
将血液从身体送回心脏的血管。Blood vessel that carries blood back to the heart from the body.
Ventricle心室
 xīn shì
心脏下部腔室，推动血液出心脏。Lower chamber of the heart that pumps blood out of the heart.

术语 (Term) 拼音 (Pinyin) 定义 (Definition)

前部的锋利牙齿，用于切割食物。Front, sharp teeth used for cutting food.
Canine犬牙
 quǎn yá
锋利尖牙，用于撕裂食物。Sharp, pointed teeth used for tearing food.
Premolar前臼齿
 qián jiù chǐ

Molar臼齿

Wisdom tooth智齿

Crown牙冠

Root牙根

Enamel牙釉质

Dentin牙本质

Pulp牙髓

Gum牙龈 / 牙肉

牙齿周围的软组织。Soft tissue surrounding the teeth.
