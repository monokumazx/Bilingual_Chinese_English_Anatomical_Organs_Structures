人体解剖器官与结构词汇表
Vocabulary List of Human Anatomical Organs and Structures

本词汇表收录了人体主要器官及解剖结构的中英文术语，附带拼音和详细定义，适合医学、健康科学及双语教学使用。内容涵盖神经系统、循环系统、呼吸系统、消化系统、生殖与泌尿系统及牙齿等多个人体系统，帮助学习者全面掌握人体解剖相关词汇。

This vocabulary list compiles the key anatomical organs and structures of the human body with terms in Chinese and English, including Pinyin and detailed definitions. It is designed for use in medicine, health sciences, and bilingual education. The content covers multiple human body systems such as the nervous, circulatory, respiratory, digestive, reproductive & urinary systems, and teeth, helping learners develop a comprehensive understanding of human anatomy terminology.

🧠 主要器官 (Major Organs)

术语 (Term) 拼音 (Pinyin) 定义 (Definition)
Brain大脑
 dà nǎo
中枢神经系统的主要部分，控制思维、情绪、感觉和动作。The main part of the central nervous system that controls thinking, emotions, sensation, and movement.
Kidney肾脏
 shèn zàng
过滤血液、产生尿液，调节体液平衡和血压。Filters blood, produces urine, regulates fluid balance and blood pressure.
Liver肝脏
 gān zàng
解毒、合成蛋白质、储存能量及代谢营养。Detoxifies, synthesizes proteins, stores energy, and metabolizes nutrients.
Lungs肺
 fèi
进行气体交换，吸入氧气，排出二氧化碳。Performs gas exchange, inhaling oxygen and exhaling carbon dioxide.
Pancreas胰腺
 yí xiàn
分泌胰岛素、消化酶，调节血糖。Secretes insulin and digestive enzymes, regulating blood sugar.
Thyroid甲状腺
 jiǎ zhuàng xiàn
产生调节新陈代谢的激素。Produces hormones that regulate metabolism.
Heart心脏
 xīn zàng
泵血器官，推动血液循环。A pumping organ that drives blood circulation.
Spleen脾脏
 pí zàng
免疫和血液过滤功能，储存白细胞和血小板。Functions in immunity and blood filtration, storing white blood cells and platelets.
Skin皮肤
 pí fū
人体最大的器官，保护身体、调节体温、感知外界刺激。The body's largest organ; protects, regulates temperature, and senses external stimuli.

🧬 生殖与泌尿系统 (Reproductive & Urinary Systems)

🧠 泌尿系统 (Urinary System)

术语 (Term) 拼音 (Pinyin) 定义 (Definition)
Ureter输尿管
 shū niào guǎn
输送尿液从肾至膀胱的管道。Tubes that transport urine from the kidneys to the bladder.
Bladder膀胱
 páng guāng
储存尿液的器官。The organ that stores urine.
Bladder Neck膀胱颈
 páng guāng jǐng
膀胱与尿道之间的连接处。The connection between the bladder and the urethra.
Urethra尿道
 niào dào
排尿通道，男性也用于排精。The tube that carries urine from the bladder to outside the body; also used for semen in males.
Adrenal gland肾上腺
 shèn shàng xiàn
位于肾上方，分泌应激激素。Located above the kidneys, secretes stress hormones.
Anus肛门
 gāng mén
排泄物排出体外的通道。The opening through which waste products are expelled from the body.

🚹 男性生殖系统 (Male Reproductive System)

术语 (Term) 拼音 (Pinyin) 定义 (Definition)
Penis阴茎
 yīn jīng
男性外生殖器官。Male external reproductive organ.
Scrotum阴囊
 yīn náng
包含睾丸的皮肤囊袋。A pouch of skin containing the testicles.
Testis睾丸
 gāo wán
产生精子和睾酮。Produces sperm and testosterone.
Seminal Vesicle精囊
 jīng náng
分泌精液的一部分。Part of the male reproductive system that secretes fluid for semen.
Prostate前列腺
 qián liè xiàn
男性生殖腺，产生前列腺液。Male reproductive gland that produces prostatic fluid.
Bulbourethral Glands尿道球腺
 niào dào qiú xiàn
分泌润滑液，清洁尿道以便精液通过。Secretes pre-ejaculate fluid to lubricate and neutralize the urethra.

🚺 女性生殖系统 (Female Reproductive System)

术语 (Term) 拼音 (Pinyin) 定义 (Definition)
Ovary卵巢
 luǎn cháo
产生卵子与女性激素的腺体。Glands that produce eggs and female hormones.
Fallopian tubes输卵管
 shū luǎn guǎn
输送卵子从卵巢至子宫的管道。Tubes that carry eggs from the ovaries to the uterus.
Uterus子宫
 zǐ gōng
胚胎发育和胎儿生长的器官。The organ where the embryo and fetus develop.
Vagina阴道
 yīn dào
连接子宫与体外的弹性肌肉管道。An elastic, muscular canal that connects the uterus to the outside of the body.
Labia Majora / Minora大阴唇 / 小阴唇
 dà yīn chún / xiǎo yīn chún
女性外生殖器的一部分，保护阴道开口。Part of the female external genitalia, protecting the vaginal opening.

❤️ 循环与呼吸系统 (Circulatory & Respiratory Systems)

术语 (Term) 拼音 (Pinyin) 定义 (Definition)
Artery动脉
 dòng mài
将血液从心脏输送到全身的血管。Blood vessel that carries blood away from the heart.
Vein静脉
 jìng mài
将血液从身体送回心脏的血管。Blood vessel that returns blood to the heart.
Ventricle心室
 xīn shì
心脏下部腔室，推动血液出心脏。Lower chamber of the heart that pumps blood.
Atrium心房
 xīn fáng
心脏上部腔室，接收血液。Upper heart chamber that receives blood.
Trachea气管
 qì guǎn
连接喉部与支气管的通道。Connects the larynx to the bronchi.
Bronchus支气管
 zhī qì guǎn
导入空气至肺部的气道。Airway leading into the lungs.
Diaphragm横膈膜
 héng gé mó
呼吸的主要肌肉，分隔胸腹腔。Main muscle of respiration.
Pleura胸膜
 xiōng mó
包裹肺部的双层膜。Double-layered membrane around the lungs.
Aorta主动脉
 zhǔ dòng mài
身体最大动脉，从心脏将血液输送至全身。The largest artery that carries blood from the heart to the body.
Capillaries毛细血管
 máo xì xuè guǎn
微细血管，进行营养和气体交换。Tiny vessels for nutrient and gas exchange.

🧠 神经与感觉系统 (Nervous & Sensory Systems)

术语 (Term) 拼音 (Pinyin) 定义 (Definition)
Cerebellum小脑
 xiǎo nǎo
协调和平衡功能的大脑部分。Coordinates movement and balance.
Spinal Cord脊髓
 jǐ suǐ
大脑与身体之间的神经通路。Nerve pathway connecting brain and body.
Hypothalamus下丘脑
 xià qiū nǎo
控制内分泌和基本行为。Controls hormones and basic drives.
Pituitary Gland脑下垂体
 nǎo xià chuí tǐ
分泌调节激素的主腺体。The master gland regulating hormones.
Medulla Oblongata延髓
 yán suǐ
控制基本生命功能，如心跳和呼吸。Controls vital functions like heart rate and breathing.
Optic Nerve视神经
 shì shén jīng
将视觉信号从眼睛传递至大脑。Transmits visual signals from the eye to the brain.
Optic Disc / Blind Spot视盘 / 盲点
 shì pán / máng diǎn
视神经进入眼球处，无感光细胞。Point where the optic nerve exits the eye; lacks photoreceptors.

🍽️ 消化系统 (Digestive System)

术语 (Term) 拼音 (Pinyin) 定义 (Definition)
Stomach胃
 wèi
初步消化食物的器官。Initial digestion of food.
Small Intestine小肠
 xiǎo cháng
吸收营养的主要部位。Main site of nutrient absorption.
Large Intestine / Colon大肠 / 结肠
 dà cháng / jié cháng
吸收水分，形成粪便。Absorbs water, forms feces.
Gallbladder胆囊
 dǎn náng
储存和浓缩胆汁。Stores and concentrates bile.
Esophagus食管
 shí guǎn
将食物从咽部输送至胃部的管道。Tube that carries food from the pharynx to the stomach.
Rectum直肠
 zhí cháng
粪便储存的末端肠道。Final segment of the large intestine where feces are stored.
Anus肛门
 gāng mén
排泄物排出体外的通道。The opening through which waste products are expelled from the body.
